Bahrain

Since I am from Bahrain this will be a special local guide from catching sunsets to exploring our deep Arab culture. Bahrain is a small island in the middle east and is generally a hot country so most of the time it is considered summer here.

Traditonal aspect


As Bahrain modernises it still tries to stay in touch with some of its great tradiotnal aspects from architecture to the food. Some places showcase both simultaenously, allowing you to really see the uniquness of Bahrain. An example of the places are "Green bar cafe" serving modern food which is located in "Qalaat al Bahrain" (also known as Bahrainfort) and has an amazing view of the sea, allowing you to have a great view especially during sunset time and with some horseriding right over the sea. The second place is "Basta" which serves traditional local food with arabic architect and interior.

Art watching

Visiting an art exhibitions and gallery is a good idea in Bahrain as they showcase many great artwork and allow you to meet local artists and explore your creative side. Having all kinds of artwork from tradional to international pieces that you can see and visit is a great activity.
