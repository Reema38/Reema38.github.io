Turkey

Turkey is a country mainly located in Western Asia, with a small portion in Southeast Europe. They are very famous for their traditional roots and stay very in touch with them most people visit as tourists but I happened to visit it for education reasons however that did not stop from being a tourist myself.

Art muesems

Turkey surprised us with the fact that they have a lot of art museums and galleries and each one had beautiful pieces of art. Art can raise serotonin levels so why not visit some in Turkey and have a new perspective of art. From graffaiti to traditional art to very empressive statues.

Scenery

Turkey have some of the most unique and eye-pleasing trails you will ever see with beautiful scenery and a peaceful walk. It is a great and common activity there espeicially if you are a nature lover or are up for a challenge.
