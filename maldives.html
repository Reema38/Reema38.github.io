Maldives

Going to the Maldives nudged us to the fact that their seasons are the opposite of our own. Summertime is their monsoon season and wintertime is when it is perfect to visit the tropical place. Maldives is the perfect getaway for some relxatiion or even adventure, you can chill by the beach and watch the sunset while sipping on something tropical or you can swim with the fish and do some water-sports either way, it is an amazing holiday spot! Since you are in an island the activities really revolve around what is surrounding you but leave it up to the Maldives to make the most out of it! and take action.

Fact


Maldives island is drowning due to global warming.
